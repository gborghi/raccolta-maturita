derivata.

f'(x) = lim
$h\to 0$
$f(x+ h) -f(x)$
= lim
$h\to 0$
$(x+ h)sen(x+ h) -x$ sen(x)

= lim
$h\to 0$
$(x+$ h)(sen(x) cos(h) + sen(h) cos(x)) $-x$ sen(x)
= lim
$h\to 0$
xsen(x) cos(h) + xsen(h) cos(x) + hsen(x) cos(h) + hsen(h) cos(x) $-x$ sen(x)

= lim
$h\to 0$
xsen(x)[cos(h) $-1] +$ xsen(h) cos(x) + hsen(x) cos(h) + hsen(h) cos(x)

= lim
$h\to 0$ [xsen(x)[cos(h) $-1]$
+ xsen(h) cos(x)
+ hsen(x) cos(h)
+ hsen(h) cos(x)

= xsen(x) $∙0 +$ xcos(x) $∙1 +$ sen(x) $+ 0 =$ xcos(x) + sen(x) = f'(x)
